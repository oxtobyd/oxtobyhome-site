Privacy Policy

Last updated:

ToDo Kanban (“the App”) is designed to store your data on your devices and in your personal iCloud account so your tasks sync between your Apple devices. We do not operate a cloud service and we do not collect your data.

Data We Store

The App stores the following user-provided content:

  • Tasks, tags, statuses, and priorities
  • Sub-tasks
  • Notes you add to tasks

This data is stored locally on your device and synced via Apple iCloud under your Apple ID using Apple frameworks.

How Sync Works

Sync is handled by Apple’s iCloud Key-Value store mechanisms. Your data is associated with your Apple ID and is not accessible to us. You can disable iCloud for the App in iOS Settings if you prefer to keep data only on-device.

Data Collection

The App does not collect personal information, analytics, or usage data. We do not track you and we do not share data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Apple iCloud is used solely to sync your data between devices you own. Apple’s privacy policy governs the handling of data stored in iCloud.

Your Choices

  • Disable iCloud for the App in iOS Settings to keep data on a single device.
  • Export your data to a JSON file within the App at any time.
  • Delete the App to remove local data from a device. Data in iCloud can be removed via iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.
